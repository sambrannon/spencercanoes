PAVILION MINIMUM PLUS FEES

The Pavilion is a large, tin-roofed area that contains a large refrigerator and work counter plus water, lights, and electricity on site.

The Pavilion's Minimum is $20.00 PLUS all regular DAY USE or OVERNIGHT fees.
