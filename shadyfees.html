BEFORE ENTERING SHADY GROVE CAMPGROUND, EVERYONE MUST REGISTER AND HANG AN ENTRANCE PERMIT ON HIS/HER VEHICLE'S REAR-VIEW MIRROR regardless of the time of day or night you arrive or whether you have reservations or not.

EVERY VEHICLE must have an Entrance Permit on its rear-view mirror. This is of utmost importance and the Permit must remain on the REAR-VIEW MIRROR at all times whether coming, going, or parked in the campground.

Anyone failing to do so may be asked to leave or be fined, banned, or towed from the premises.
