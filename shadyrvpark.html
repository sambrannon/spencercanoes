RV Park

We recently installed 3 full (20-30-50) RV hook-ups on a hill within Shady Grove Campground. You have full access to the river and all of the common areas and trails in the campground. We have plans for a few more hook-ups in the future.

All the CAMPGROUND RULES apply except the Midnight Curfew, but any activity must be inside your RV. No outside activity (fires, etc.) after midnight. Sound carries a remarkable distance in the campground so your consideration of your tent-camping and RV neighbors will be appreciated.

  • Full Hook-up Rates
    • $30.00 per day for one or two persons. Includes all utilities and your Entrance Permit. Additional persons in the RV will pay the regular camping fees quoted for Overnight or Day Use rates.

  • Monthly Rates
    • $250.00 per month plus your electricity for fall and winter months for one or two adults only. Water and trash pick-up is included in the monthly fee. All sites have electric meters.

      On a monthly basis, we are not set up to accommodate pets nor children under the age of 18 years. Any visitors to your site will pay the regular Overnight or Day Use fees, whichever is appropriate.

  • Weekly Rates
    • $150.00 per week for one or two persons. Additional persons will pay the regular Overnight and Day Use fees.
