Registration is SELF-SERVICE and easily possible day or night. All the materials are in a BOX to the right of the RED DOOR on the PORCH of the OFFICE. The porch light is always on so visibility is never a problem. Directions are posted on the wall above the box, but the procedure is fairly straight-forward.

The box contains registration envelopes, brochures, and pens. Every numbered envelope contains an Entrance Permit with a corresponding number and each envelope must be filled out completely with your name, vehicle description, name of your site if you know it, number of guests in each vehicle, etc., etc.

FUSHIA envelopes are filled out by OVERNIGHT guests and bright YELLOW envelopes by DAY USE and RIVER ACCESS guests. Fees and blanks are printed on the envelopes to help you figure out how much money to put in the envelope. Take the Permit out, put your CASH OR CHECK ONLY in the envelope, seal it and put it through the mail slot in the red door. THEN, HANG YOUR PERMIT ON YOUR REAR-VIEW MIRROR BEFORE YOU DRIVE DOWN THE HILL TO THE CAMPGROUND. BEFORE, PLEASE!

In the case of more than one vehicle in the same group where one person is going to pay for all camping fees, everyone in the group must still fill out an envelope regarding his vehicle and the number of persons in it, etc., but in the blank "Amount Sealed in Envelope", simply write the name of the group or the name of the site or the person paying for everyone and hang your Entrance Permit on your rear-view mirror, please.

If requested in plenty of time, a number of envelopes can be mailed to the organizer of a large group so that each driver can then fill out his envelope ahead of time and drop it through the mail slot upon his arrival at Shady Grove.
